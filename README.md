# Kitchen Butler: Your Personal Kitchen ERP

![Kitchen Butler Logo](https://via.placeholder.com/150)

Kitchen Butler is a cutting-edge SaaS application designed to revolutionize how you manage your kitchen. From inventory tracking to meal planning, Kitchen Butler is your all-in-one solution for a smarter, more efficient kitchen experience.

## 🌟 Key Features (MVP)

- **Inventory Management**: Keep track of all your ingredients and never run out of essentials again.
- **Recipe Organization**: Store and manage your favorite recipes in one place.
- **Smart Meal Planning**: Plan your meals for the week and automatically generate shopping lists.
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Intuitive design for seamless kitchen management.
- **Secure Authentication**: Keep your culinary data safe and accessible only to you.

## 🚀 Roadmap

We're committed to continuously improving Kitchen Butler. Here's what's on the horizon:

### Phase 2: Nutritional Insights

- Add nutritional data to ingredients and recipes
- Provide nutritional summaries for meal plans
- Implement smart recipe suggestions based on available ingredients
- Introduce multiple user profiles for family accounts

### Phase 3: Smart Shopping

- Integrate with online grocery services for automated ordering
- Enhance meal planning with AI-powered suggestions
- Implement seasonal recipe recommendations

### Phase 4: Connected Kitchen

- Integrate with smart kitchen appliances for a truly connected experience
- Introduce community features for recipe sharing and reviews

### Phase 5: Beyond the Kitchen

- Restaurant integration for balanced eat-out options
- Advanced analytics for personalized insights on eating habits and kitchen efficiency

### Phase 6: Dietary Management

- Allow users to set dietary preferences (vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free, etc.)
- Implement allergy tracking and alerts
- Provide personalized recipe recommendations based on dietary needs

### Phase 7: Financial Insights

- Track grocery expenses over time
- Set and monitor food budgets
- Provide cost breakdowns for meals and recipes

### Phase 8: Sustainability and Waste Reduction

- Suggest recipes to use up ingredients before they expire
- Track and analyze food waste
- Provide tips for proper food storage to extend shelf life
- Calculate the carbon footprint of meals
- Suggest eco-friendly alternatives for ingredients

### Phase 9: Smart Home Integration

- Voice control through smart speakers (Alexa, Google Home)
- Integration with smart scales for automatic inventory updates
- Notifications through smart displays

### Phase 10: Social Culinary Network

- Allow users to share recipes and meal plans with friends or family
- Implement a rating and review system for recipes
- Create public profiles for users who want to showcase their culinary creations

### Phase 11: Advanced Recipe Management

- Automatic recipe scaling based on desired servings
- Recipe version control (save modifications and variations)
- Interactive cooking instructions with timers and step-by-step guidance

### Phase 12: Health and Wellness

- Integration with fitness apps to align meals with exercise routines
- Track macronutrients and micronutrients over time
- Set and monitor health goals (e.g., reducing sugar intake, increasing protein)

### Phase 13: Kitchen Equipment Optimization

- Inventory and track kitchen tools and appliances
- Provide maintenance reminders and tips
- Suggest recipes based on available equipment

### Phase 14: AI-Powered Personal Chef Assistant

- Use machine learning to understand user preferences and habits
- Provide personalized cooking tips and technique improvements
- Offer real-time cooking assistance through image recognition

## 🛠 Tech Stack

- Backend: Go with Fiber and Gorm
- Frontend: Next.js
- Database: PostgreSQL
- Payment Processing: Stripe
